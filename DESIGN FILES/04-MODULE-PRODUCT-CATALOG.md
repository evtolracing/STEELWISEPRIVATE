# 04 - PRODUCT CATALOG & MATERIALS MODULE

## Module Overview
Central repository for steel products, grades, specifications, and material science data.

---

## FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

| ID | Requirement | Priority |
|----|-------------|----------|
| PC-01 | Maintain steel grade library with chemistry/mechanical specs | Critical |
| PC-02 | Define products with dimensional ranges and pricing | Critical |
| PC-03 | Support ASTM/ISO/MIL/SAE specification standards | Critical |
| PC-04 | Track grade equivalencies across standards | High |
| PC-05 | Attach specification documents (PDFs) | High |
| PC-06 | Support custom customer specifications | Medium |
